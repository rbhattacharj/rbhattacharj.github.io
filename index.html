Research Interests

I'm broadly interested in theoretical computer science and machine learning. Specifically I work in the following areas:

  • Randomized Algorithms for problems in Linear Algebra, Graph Theory and Machine Learning.
  • Online Learning and Optimization. Specifcially, online caching algorithms with sublinear regret and matching lower bounds.
  • Designing algorithms with machine-learned advice.
Recently, some fo the problems I've been working on include estimating eignevalues of matrices in sublinear time, online caching algorithms with machine learned advice and estimating maximum mathcing in graph streams in sublinear space. Previously, I've also worked on online algorithms for minimizing the age-of-information in communicaton networks.
